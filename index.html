Training Program Expectations

    Throughout the course of the training I expect to be introduced to techniques and technologies related to variety of robotics topics such as mobile robots, robotics manipulators and drones. I expect the training program will cover the relevant skills and knowledge through project-based learning approach, which will facilitate the learning process and equip us with hands-on experience while dealing with real robotic platforms.
